Welcome to South Africa

South Africa is located at the southernmost region of Africa, with a long coastline that stretches more than 2,500 km and along two oceans (the South Atlantic and the Indian). South Africa is the 25th-largest country in the world and is comparable in size to Colombia. Mafadi in the Drakensberg at 3,450 m (11,320 ft) is the highest peak in South Africa.


There are a million activities for young and old in South Africa for everyone to try. Kite surfing, hiking, safari, and more are all at your finger tips in SA. So come, stay a while in SA!
